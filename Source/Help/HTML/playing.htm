How to Play BoobyTrap!

You start a new game of BoobyTrap! by clicking the New Game button (a new game is automatically started when you load the program).

If you think a square is safe you "step" on it by left-clicking it. If it's booby trapped it "explodes" and you loose the game. If the square is not booby trapped then it reveals some information to help you find booby trapped squares. If there are booby traps in squares adjacent to the one you step on the square displays to number of adjacent booby traps. If there a no adjacent squares with booby traps then the square and all surrounding squares are revealed until squares that have bounding booby traps are displayed.

You can mark a square that you think is booby trapped by placing a "flag" on it. You do this by right-clicking the square. This marks the square with a skull and crossbones "flag". Un-flag a flagged square by right clicking it again. You have as many flags at your disposal as there are booby trapped squares. You win the game by correctly flagging all the booby trapped squares.

If you're unsure about a square and want to return to it later you can place a query sign on in by holding down the SHIFT key and right clicking it. This places a question mark symbol on the square. You remove the mark by repeating the action.

If you loose the game by stepping on a booby trapped square the program shows you all the booby trapped squares – the ones you correctly flagged are shown in red, the ones you didn't find in black and the ones you incorrectly flagged are shown with a green cross.

You can surrender at any time after starting to play by clicking the Surrender button. This will display the location of all the booby traps – colour coded as described above.

Your game is timed. If you win and your time is one of the three fastest times then your name and time is placed on a leader board.
